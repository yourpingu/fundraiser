How to donate with Skrill

Step 1: Create an account

First you will need to follow the link above and go to the register form. Then fill in your details and select your correct country and currency.

Step 2: Complete registration

Once you're on the dashboard, select "Send Money".

Select "Get Started".

Skrill will ask you to complete your registration, fill in your adress and phone number.

Step 3: Deposit Money

Once you've completed your registration, Skrill will give you different deposit options depending on where you are located.

Select your preferred option and follow the given instructions.

Final Step: Send money

Choose to send money to another Skrill account and fill in viktorhura.info@gmail.com

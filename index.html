Nyx's journey in Bolivia

The Janajpacha School of Life is a self-sustaining eco-village located in Cochabamba, Bolivia.
This space offers the opportunity to deliver professional services in exchange for accommodation, food and education.
However, in the face of such a change in scenario, there are many more expenses to cover: plane tickets, legal paperwork for the professional stay, the necessary updating of work tools to be able to fulfill the commitment, clothing, tools, proof of COVID-19 and vaccinations required by the Bolivian government, and even the move from Asunción to Capiatá that must be covered in the first instance, before moving to Bolivia.

In addition, the pending investment in basic audio recording tools, since this trip is also to develop a solo musical career, with a flute and voices, long dreamed of.
In addition to a myriad of investments that are individually small but that, added together, mean larger numbers.

Nyx is a graphic designer and digital communications strategist, as well as a writer, poet, illustrator, and musician.
This change in employment perspective has been taking place for almost three years in action, and a lifetime in spirit, and the economic challenge has always been present since Paraguay is an expensive and cruel country for artists.

That is why we appeal to the solidarity of friends to help Nyx follow her life path, which comes from a deep spiritual calling, and thus be able to return to the world all the abundance, light and wisdom that she has received throughout the years.
